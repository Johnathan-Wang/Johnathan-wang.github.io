HoloHands

A VR game that is designed to teach basic Singapore sign language in an immersive and engaging way. By leveraging hand tracking technology, users can learn and practice signs through interactive lessons and real-time feedback. The goal is to make sign language more accessible to a wider audience, promoting inclusivity and communication for the hearing-impaired community in singapore.
